Feed the Animals!


  • Feel the excitement as the animals eat out of your hand. All proceeds will help to fund our wildlife conservation efforts in the region.
  • Elephant Feeding
  • Girrafe feeding
  • Goat Feeding
  • Giant Tortoise Feeding

Splash Safari Show!

  • Witness our sea lion gracefully glide on water, effortlessly balance a ball on its nose as well as many special moments that will tickle the bottow of your heart!
  • Located at the Shaw Foundation Amphitheatre
  • Showtimes:10.30am & 5pm

Wildlife Tour!


  • Embark on a free and easy tour with your personal guide on your own private buggy.
  • Up to 6 pax.
  • Public ($) : 300 per 1.5 HR
    Member ($) : 240 per 1.5 HR
  • For further enquires, please email us at tours.zoo@wrs.com.sg!

Elephant Presentation!

  • An educational experience that aims to foster and convey conservation awareness. Come find out how intelligent, sociable, strong and yet gentle elephants really are!
  • Located at Elephants of Asia
  • Showtimes : 11.30am & 3.30pm
